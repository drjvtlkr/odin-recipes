Vegan Indian Curry with Cauliflower and Lentils


Vegan Indian Curry with Cauliflower and Lentils

Description

This recipe is what I belive is so common at my place that I eat it atleast once a week at my home. My mother makes the best Vegan Indian Curry with Cauliflower and Lentils and it tastes like heaven.

Ingredients

  1. 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  2. 1 onion, finely chopped
  3. 1 (4 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  4. 1 large clove garlic, minced
  5. 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  6. 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  7. ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  8. ½ cup red lentils
  9. ½ cup red lentils
  10. 1 head cauliflower, cut into small florets
  11. 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  12. 1 large carrot, peeled and diced
  13. ½ cup frozen green beans, thawed
  14. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  15. 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  16. 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  17. 1 sprig fresh cilantro

    18. Steps

    1. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large saucepan over low heat and cook onion, stirring frequently, until soft and translucent about 10 minutes. Add ginger, garlic, coriander, cumin, and turmeric and cook, stirring continuously, for 2 minutes. Stir in lentils and pour in vegetable stock. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer gently for 10 minutes.
    2. Meanwhile, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook cauliflower until lightly browned, 2 to 3 minutes.
    3. Stir cauliflower, coconut milk, and carrot into the lentil mixture. Bring the curry back to a gentle simmer and cook until vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes. Stir in green beans and cook for an additional 3 to 4 minutes.
    4. Stir 3 tablespoons cilantro and lemon juice into the curry. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon onto a warmed serving dish and garnish with cilantro spring.
