Vegan Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry


Vegan Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry

Description

I have never eaten a sweet potato curry with Chickpea. Although the fact that it is vegan is good news, as it happens that I am trying to move towards vegetarianism.

Ingredients

  1. 3 tablespoons olive oil
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  5. 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained
  6. 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  7. 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  8. 1 sweet potato, cubed
  9. 1 tablespoon garam masala
  10. 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  11. 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  12. ½ teaspoon salt
  13. ¼ teaspoon red chile flakes
  14. 1 cup baby spinach

    15. Steps

    1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook onion, garlic, and ginger until softened, about 5 minutes. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, and sweet potato. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
    2. Season with garam masala, cumin, turmeric, chile flakes, and salt. Add spinach right before serving.
